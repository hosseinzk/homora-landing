Redefining Home Services in Uusimaa

Homora was founded with a simple belief: every home deserves professional care, delivered with precision, reliability, and respect.

With over 15 years of experience in luxury hospitality standards, we bring the same level of quality, consistency, and attention to detail into homes across Uusimaa. We are fully insured, background-checked, and committed to building a trusted service brand in Finland — one home at a time.

Our Story

Two needs, one mission

Homora was created to bridge two important needs in Finland: Reliable, high-quality home services and Meaningful job opportunities for immigrants and students.

Our founder's background in luxury hotel operations shaped the core philosophy of Homora: service should be systematic, consistent, and quality-controlled — not random or unpredictable. From the beginning, our goal was not just to clean homes, but to build a trusted service company that families can rely on long-term.

Today, Homora supports households across Helsinki, Espoo, Vantaa and all major cities in Uusimaa — offering professional cleaning, laundry & ironing, carpet cleaning, tailoring services, handyman assistance, and moving support. We are still growing. And we are building something sustainable — not just fast.
