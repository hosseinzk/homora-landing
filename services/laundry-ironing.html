Frequently asked questions

What is one laundry load?
One load equals 4 kg of laundry. This is approximately 15-20 regular clothing items or 2-3 sets of bed linens.
Do you handle special textiles like silk or wool?
Yes, but special textiles are priced after inspection. Drop off items for assessment, and we'll provide a quote based on fabric care requirements.
How long does wash and fold take?
Standard turnaround is 24-48 hours. Express same-day service may be available for an additional fee — contact us to confirm.
Do you provide pickup and delivery?
Yes! We offer pickup and delivery for laundry services within Helsinki Metropolitan Area. Travel zone fees apply for areas beyond central Helsinki.
How much does laundry service cost near me?
Pricing depends on the size of your order and the services selected. You'll always see clear pricing with no hidden fees before confirming your booking.
What is included in your laundry service?
Our laundry service includes washing, drying, and ironing. Your clothes are returned clean, fresh, and ready to wear.
