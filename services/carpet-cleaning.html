Cleaning time depends on the size and condition of the carpet. In most cases, the cleaning process is completed within a few hours, and drying time is fast thanks to our professional equipment.

Most carpets dry within a few hours. Our advanced extraction process removes most of the moisture, allowing for quick drying and faster use.

Yes, we use eco-friendly and non-toxic cleaning solutions that are safe for both pets and children.

Yes, we clean a wide range of carpets, including wool, synthetic, viscose, and delicate rugs. Each carpet is treated with the appropriate cleaning method.
